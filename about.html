Adopt, don't shop!

Adopting means giving a second chance to an animal in need, and by doing so, you're helping create a kinder, more sustainable community here in Gothenburg.

At Pet Tales Göteborg, we’re passionate about matching you with the perfect pet. Whether you’re looking for a furry friend or a scaly companion, we’re here to guide you every step of the way. Join us in making a difference—adopt, don’t shop!

The Tale of How Pet Tales Came to Be

Pet Tales Göteborg began as a heartfelt dream born out of a passion for animals and the belief that every pet, regardless of their shape or size, deserves a second chance at a loving home. Our founder, inspired by the diversity of animals in need—from furry cats and dogs to reptiles and small critters—envisioned a place where every type of pet could find their perfect human match. This dream became a reality in Gothenburg, a city known for its strong sense of community and commitment to sustainability.

As animal lovers, we knew that adopting rather than shopping for pets could not only change the lives of the animals we rescue but also help reduce the harmful demand for commercial breeding. With that belief, Pet Tales Göteborg was founded to connect people with pets in need, creating a space where love, care, and companionship could flourish.

Our journey began with just a handful of local volunteers who shared our vision. Today, we’re proud to work alongside the broader Gothenburg community—creating a network of pet owners, rescuers, and supporters who are dedicated to giving every pet a safe and loving home. At Pet Tales, we’ve helped countless animals write the next chapter in their lives, and we’re excited to continue building a future where every pet has a story worth telling.

Join us in making a difference. One tale at a time.
