The Team Behind Pet Tales

At Pet Tales Göteborg, we believe that every animal deserves a loving home. By choosing to adopt, you're not only gaining a loyal companion but also giving a second chance to pets in need. Our mission is to connect you with pets of all kinds—whether furry or scaly—that are ready to be part of your family.

Located in the heart of Gothenburg, we work hand-in-hand with the local community to ensure that every pet finds a safe and caring home. Gothenburg is a city that values sustainability, and adopting a pet is a meaningful way to support this ethos—helping reduce the demand for commercial breeding while giving back to a community that loves its animals.

Join us in making a difference. Adopt, don’t shop, and be part of a caring network that’s transforming lives, one adoption at a time.
